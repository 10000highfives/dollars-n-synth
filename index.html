A synth about $$

Did you ever wish you could play a synth while a philospher talked about $?

you can play this synth with your computer keyboard and/or mouse while Michael Sandel speaks in his melodious tenor.

  • Click the "$" inside the box to turn on Mr. Sandel.
  • This is best on a computer--likely spotty on mobile
  • This is best on a modern web browser like Firefox or Chrome, not Internet Explorer or Safari.
